ABOUT ME

Engineer by Profession. Endurance Athlete by Passion.

My running journey began in 2014 after participating in the CRPF Half Marathon and Airtel Delhi Half Marathon. What started as curiosity soon became a lifelong pursuit of endurance.

A few friends from my cricket circle introduced me to distance running. Their encouragement inspired me to attempt longer races, and eventually ultramarathons became my true passion.

Running taught me far more than speed or distance. It taught discipline, patience, resilience and humility. Every ultra is an opportunity to understand yourself a little better and discover strengths you never knew existed.

Professionally I work as a Staff Engineer at Samsung Research, while outside work I dedicate my time to endurance sports, community building and mentoring new runners.

Through my running community, Spirits of Runners, I enjoy helping beginners discover running, support experienced runners, and create an environment where people of all ages can enjoy fitness together.
